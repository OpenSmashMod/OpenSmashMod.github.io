Contribute to OSM!

We need people to contribute to OSM! If you have good ideas and want to help out, then head on over to our GitHub Repository to contribute!

How do I contribute?

If you don't know how to contribute, there's a guide over on our Discord Server! Head on over there to check out the guide, and we're there to help you out if you need any extra help.
OSM is a non-profit, open-source mod of Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and 3DS.
The OSM Development Team (OSMDT) does not support any form of piracy; The official versions of OSM require all users to own a copy of the game for either system. Additionally, no paid DLC will be unlocked by using this mod.
