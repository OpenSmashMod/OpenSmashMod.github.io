What is OSM?

OSM is a new -potentially competitive- mod for Super Smash Bros. 3DS and Wii U. As this mod is open-source, the only limits are the community's imagination! Some things that have the potential to change include Characters, Gameplay, Stages, SFX and Music, Skins, and even more! If there's something not yet possible to edit, just let us know and we'll do our best to make it editable for you!

OSM is a non-profit, open-source mod of Super Smash Bros. for Wii U and 3DS.
The OSM Development Team (OSMDT) does not support any form of piracy; The official versions of OSM require all users to own a copy of the game for either system. Additionally, no paid DLC will be unlocked by using this mod.
